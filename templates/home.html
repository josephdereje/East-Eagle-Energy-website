About Us

Powering Ethiopia Since 2022

East Eagle Energy, established in 2022, specializes in inverters, battery packs, and comprehensive solar energy systems designed to deliver dependable power solutions.

The company focuses on providing stable energy storage units for both residential and commercial projects, ensuring clients have consistent, reliable access to power regardless of grid conditions.

Our mission: supply energy solutions that remain resilient and dependable over time — Energy That Never Grows Weary.
